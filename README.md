## Planet Argon and the Second Act work

### When we’re usually pulled in

- The Rails app still powers the business… but upgrades have stalled for years.
- “We should rewrite” keeps winning… because nobody trusts the current system.
- Audit, compliance, or customer pressure is rising… and old versions are now a risk.
- Team turnover erased context… and the codebase feels expensive to touch.
- Performance and delivery speed are slipping… and every change costs more than it should.

### How we help

- **Code audits + upgrade roadmaps**: de-risk the path forward with an actionable plan.
- **Modernization support**: incremental upgrades, refactors, and capability work.
- **Coaching**: help teams build the muscle to maintain the system long-term.

### What working with us looks like

I’m often the first conversation. Then we bring in the right engineer or pair from Planet Argon to do the work… and we stay accountable through delivery.
